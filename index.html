About Alif Online Academy

Alif Online Academy is dedicated to providing high-quality Islamic education through online platforms. We specialize in teaching Quran Hifz (memorization), Tajweed (proper recitation), and Tilawa (beautiful recitation) to ladies and children in a comfortable, supportive, and nurturing environment.

Our qualified instructors are experienced in Quranic sciences and are committed to helping students develop a strong connection with the Holy Quran. We understand the unique needs of female students and children, providing a safe and encouraging learning space. Classes are conducted in Malayalam and Urdu, making learning comfortable and easy to understand.
